ABOUT US



Steellun is the official Material Advantage chapter of Department of Metallurgical and Materials Engineering NIT Rourkela, established in the year 2019

OUR EVENTS


Nano Tech Seminar

Steellun-NITR kickstarts it's first talk on the overly spreaded research topic of Nanotechnology by Dr. Jay Chakraborty, Principal Scientist, National Metallurgical Laboratory. Dr. Chakraborty's experience on the subject and knowledge of the area will surely be helpful for the research enthusiast people.


Space Hunt

Steellun NITR presents “Space Hunt”- a heated treasure hunt where teams shall battle it out to emerge at the top. Each team will have to solve a set of 4 questions that shall lead them to the next location inside the vast Academic Block. The locations will have QR codes, which when scanned shall present a new set of questions. These questions will act as clues for the next location.


Growing Innovators

Steellun NITR organised the first K-12 outreach program of the academic year 2019-20 "Growing Innovators"on 8th of September.The theme for the event was "Disaster Management and Climate Change". The participants for the event were selected on the basis of their performance in an aptitude test conducted by the Chapter at various schools of Rourkela.


EDGE 2k20

Edge is the flagship event of Material Advantage chapter NITR and its organised to give platform to all students to showcase their scientific, technical, diplomatic and soft skills in a two days extravaganza events.
